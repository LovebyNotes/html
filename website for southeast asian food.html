Filipino Chicken Adobo

One of the most amazing Asian chicken recipes. Intense in flavour, but so fast and easy to prepare!
Chicken is so tender, it's like it's been slow-cooked.

Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

  • 750g/1.5lb chicken thigh fillets
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup (85ml) soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 4 bay leaves

For Cooking

  • 2tbsp oil
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 small brown onion diced
  • 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp whole black pepper

Serving

  • 2 green onion sliced

Instructions

  1. Combine Chicken and Marinade ingredients in a bowl. Marinate for at least 20
    minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat. Remove chicken from marinade
    (reserve marinade)and place in the pan. Sear both sides until browned-
    about 1 minute on each side. Please don't cook the chicken all the way through.
  3. Remove chicken skillet and set aside.
  4. Heat the remaining oil in the skillet. Add garlic and onion, cook 1/2 minute.
  5. Add the reserved marinade, water, sugar and black pepper. Please bring it to a
    simmer, then turn the heat down to medium-high. Simmer 5 minutes.
  6. If the sauce isn't thick enough, remove chicken onto a plate and let the sauce
    simmer by itself- it will thicken much quciker- then return chicken to the
    skillet to coat in the glaze.
  7. Coat chicken in glaz,e then serve over rice. Pictured in post as a healthy dinner
    plate(415 calories)with cauliflower rice and Ginger Smashed Cucumbers

Recipe Notes

  1. Chicken thighs- do NOT substitute with breast.You need the fat in thighs in
    order for the sauce to reduce to a glaze. Can also use bone in thighs,wings
    or drumsticks(add 3/4 cup water and simmer 30 minutes).
  2. Soy sauce- use all purpose or light soy sauce.Do not use dark soy sauce-
    bottle will be labelled as such if it is. Dark soy is too intense for this sauce once
    reduced
  3. Nutrition-per serving, chicken only and assumes all sauce consumed, which is
    unlikely as it's quite strong. Reduce sodium by using low sodium soy.

Make a low calorie dinner plate with a side of Cauliflower Rice and Smashed
Cucumbers - total 415 calories for dinner plate.
